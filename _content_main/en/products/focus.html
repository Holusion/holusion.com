360° vue

The Focus is a high definition hologram, also called Spy Pepper's Ghost. The image appears in suspension at 360° : so you can visualize your product, your work or your project under all his angles. The holographic applications are numerous and innovative. They allow many uses in formation, 3D simulation and augmented reality. These business applications are ariving in special events, retail, architecture, museology, medicine, etc.
