Give a new dimension to your objects

Flash is a device allowing to present objects behind a transparent lcd screen. The informations about the product directly appear overlayed on the glass : texts, zooms on the object, explanatory videos, animations and 3D modeling… These glasses can be tailor made to fit a commercial purpose or a museography context. Ranging from 21 to 60 inches, they present an excellent rendering of colors in full HD and a unique transparency. The informations can be sent automatically (plug and play) but you can also interact in real time with it (press buttons, nfc sensor…).

This technology was on Museum Connections in january 2016. In partnership with EDIKOM and VIRTUAVISION
