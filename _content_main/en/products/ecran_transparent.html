Give a new dimension to your objects

The transparent window is a device allowing to present objects behind a transparent lcd screen. The object-related informations directly appear superimposed on the glass : texts, zooms on the object, explanatory videos, animations and 3D modeling… These glasses can be made tailored to adapt to a universe of museography or digital commerce. Ranging from 21 to 60 inches, they present an excellent rendering of colors in full HD and a unparalleled transparency. The informations can be sent automatically (plug and play) but you can also interact in real time with them (boutton poussoir, capteur nfc…).

This technology was on Museum Connections in january 2016. In partnership with EDIKOM and VIRTUAVISION
