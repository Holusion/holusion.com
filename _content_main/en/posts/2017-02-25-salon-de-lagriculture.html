Hauts-de-France cultural heritage sublimated by holograms

From the 25th of February to the 5th of March 2017, the Focus was present on the Hauts-de-France booth at the International Agricultural Show, Paris Porte de Versailles. Inside the Hall 3, every region was presented their culinary traditions, but also cultural heritage and reasons to come visits each other. .

Révélation des plus beaux monuments

After seing the hologram, the visitor can manipulate several building printed in 3D. By presenting it in front of the holographic display, he can see the same building appearing in 3D with extra annotations and explanations. Magic ? No, NFC communication !

In partnership with UPJV - Université de Picardie Jules Verne for its work on digitalization of Amiens Cathedral and holograms by Achelle3D. Tri-D for 3D printing!
