Dentsu seizes the hologram for its 2017 greetings

Dentsu Aegis Network, a communication agency, wanted to be remembered on this beginning of year 2017. So they chose the Pixel to reveal a personalized and dynamic message. The famous emojis have wished happiness, love, festivity but also success and growth of everybody.

The Pixel in the spotlight

The holographic goodies fulfilled its mission : reach the homes of all the clients and coworkers of Dentsu. A small playful object, custom abd innovative, the Pixel made a very good impression in everyone's mind on this new year.

Others made it !

Many agences chose this innovative object to present their greetings to their clients. Collaborators of Yoo Communication Interactive and of Reflet Vidéo have chosen to appear in the Pixel with a little greetings speech and a dynamic and colorful realisation.
