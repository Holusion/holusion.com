Enhanced protection for safe holographic display

The must-have for a truly successful display: the plexiglass cover for holographic fans is designed to effectively protect your holographic fan, whether during transport or operation. Thanks to its compact and transparent design, the cover fulfills its role discreetly, offering an immersive and safe show for your booth and your visitors. Thanks to its custom-made and insulating build, the protective cover also dampens the noise produced by the fan's motor, ensuring a most professional and remarkable demonstration.

Two cover formats for a custom showcase

To ensure a demonstration as close as possible to your needs, you can choose the format of your protective cover, available among these options: -The round cover: sleek and discreet, its design is ideal for delivering a stunning holographic projection in complete safety. -The square cover: durable and stable, it lets you place the fan directly on a flat surface without fixing, for an even more optimized display.
