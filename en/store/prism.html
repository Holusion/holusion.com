Features of the Prism

The illusion of the floating picture inside of the glass pyramid will draw your customer’s attention so that you can start a conversation about the product displayed in the Prism. You can customise the content and create a 3D picture that perfectly suits you. The hologram is visible over 360° so you can place the Prism in the middle of a room and let people see the hologram from each side of the Prism.

Dimensions and transport

The Prism is 44cm(L) by 37cm(D) by 22cm(H) so it can be installed almost everywhere. Its simple design makes it easy to add in any kind of setup. Moreover, the Prism only weighs 7kg and can be transported easily. It is entirely movable and plug&play which is perfect if you need to move to another point of sale or exhibition.

Pepper's ghost illusion

The hologram exists because of the Pepper's ghost optical illusion . There is a HD screen with high brightness on the top of the Prism, it projects four pictures that are identical but independent downward, one on each face of the pyramid. It creates the illusion of a 3D picture that levitates in the pyramid, thanks to the optical illusion.

Functioning of the Prism

The holographic showcase works thanks to a minicomputer which allows you to manage your content remotely directly with your smartphone, tablet or computer, using the Holusion Companion app. You can also connect your Prism to a mini WIFI router thanks to an Ethernet cable. Doing so, you will be able to easily change the content of the Prism, online, as much as you want and in a few seconds only.

Each Prism can store hundreds of 3D models and you can display your own creations ! Hence, you can present your products and information about them, in a fascinating 3D display. Thereby, you will have an innovative and interactive medium to strengthen your speeches and enthral your audience.

Finally, you can program the Prism by yourself thanks to the available API. There are four USB ports on the holographic showcase so that you can plug various sensors (touch-sensitive screen, NFC sensor…) to enhance the experience even more.

The prism offers plenty of fascinating options for 3D display. Let your imagination go and offer your customers a unique holographic experience.
