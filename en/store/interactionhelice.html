Innovative interaction with your holographic fan

The two optional boxes making up the Interactive Box and the Audio Box allow for the pairing of a holographic fan with any compatible external peripheral to guarantee even deeper interactivity between your holographic fan and its viewer. The Interactive Box will allow your target to become a true actor in the holographic projection, enabling them to interact with the video via a system of buttons, levers, and any other innovative systems... The Audio Box will add a welcome touch of sound to your hologram by allowing the coupling of your fan to an external speaker, ensuring perfect synchronicity between sound and image.

Please note that external peripherals, such as remote controls and speakers to be paired, are not included in the proposal.
