Usage

Equipped with a touch screen firmly attached to its stand and a more powerful embedded computer, turn your hologram into an interactive installation.

You can display interactive 3D scenes and highlight your content with guided tours, explanatory articles or spatialized annotations created on eCorpus.

Contents

The kit consists of a 21" multi-touch capacitive touch screen, a more powerful embedded computer capable of displaying 3D scenes in real time, and a stand connecting the whole to the hologram structure or its stele.

{% picture grid img/products/touch_support.webp --alt a touch screen stand designed to connect it to a structure to form a coherent whole %}

Designed to attach to the steles and structures of our products, this stand ties the whole installation together while offering clean cable routing.
