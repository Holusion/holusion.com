Interactive

A presentation and collaboration tool, the touch table is an ideal support for free or guided presentations. It allows you to present a large amount of content in an interactive and intuitive way.

Its 4K display and multi-touch capacitive interface make it perfect for presenting complex projects such as real estate projects, or interactive maps.

It is the ideal mediation support for complex digitized collections.

Sturdy and versatile

IP54 (Front) certified ; the screen is protected by a 3mm tempered glass (hardness 7H).

Our tactile offer benefits from all the tools and solutions of interactive software developed by Holusion. It is also possible to synchronize the table with a remote screen or hologram from our range.

Our table's design makes it accessible to people of all ages and allows it to be used by people with reduced mobility.
