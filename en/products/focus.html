360° view

The Focus is a high definition hologram, also called Spy Pepper's Ghost. The image appears in suspension at 360° : so you can visualize your product, your work or your project under all his angles. Each face of the Focus can display a different content to facilitate the interaction with your customers and collaborators.

Multiple use cases

The holographic applications are numerous and innovative. They allow many uses in formation, 3D simulation and augmented reality. These business applications are ariving in special events, retail, architecture, museology, medicine, etc.

The Focus is available worldwide to buy or to rent.
