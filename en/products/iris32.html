Made for you

The IRIS holographic display is full of potential : show your products, present your project in high definition, optimize your workspace. This solution is easy to custom and to blend in your own environment. Thanks to its interactivity, the IRIS is the perfect educational support for your corporate presentation or teaching.

Object mapping

Blend your product inside the hologram ! Any object place inside the holographic chamber will be subject to holographic video mapping. Special effects, customization, side properties revelation, a lot of graphic tools are here to help you highlight your product story.
