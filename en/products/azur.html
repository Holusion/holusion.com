180° hologram

The Azur diffuses a hologram in its three faced pyramid of glass. Its transparency gives you the feeling of being able to grasp the 3D object that appears in front of you.

We support you in the creation of this captivating object and we guide you in the choice of interactive applications that your customers will be able to handle in real time. The Azur is a mobile and plug & play product which adapts easily to all your projects.
