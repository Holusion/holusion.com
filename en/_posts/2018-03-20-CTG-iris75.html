A new experience in Luxembourg, starting with an Opal and ending with an Iris 75.

CTG, awarded with the Great Place to Work label, is a major supplier of digital services since more than 20 years in Luxembourg and 50 years worldwide. This company invested a lot on Human Ressources, carrer tracking and positive work environnement.

This fundamental values of CTG were highlighted in an XXL holograms displaying coworkers of the company telling you about their daily life. Our very first contact was trough the Opal H . As it was sucess, we carry on and collaborate with Maud and Justine Clavier, very gifted video making sisters, to go directly to Luxembourg to records the interviews in CTG.

The coworkers told about their experience at CTG. This holographic video was used at the Moovijob Tour in Luxembourg in March 2018.

Thanks to Dhanna, communication manager at CTG, for the time, skills and joy she invested in the project. Merci Thanks to Maud and Justine for this collaboration.
