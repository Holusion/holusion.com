Hologram in showroom

For the marketing launch of a new complex Les portes du chapitre, Pierre de Seine of the Rouen-based group At'ome trusted Holusion.

By acquiring an holographic showcase : l'Iris 46 they have a new presentation tool for their models.

Thanks to 3D modeling and transparence effect, the hologram makes it possible to discover facets of the building that cannot be seen on other media. Moreover, shows contents follow, reinforce and illustrate the business strategy.

Hologram is an inovating tool for real estate presentation.

Holographic real estate presentation

The holographic model replaces the physical model and shoxs project caracteristics :

  • Rotating view of the exterior of the complex with its modern architecture and large terraces
  • Visualisation of the flats on each floor
  • Practical information: number of parking spaces, neighbourhood environment, total area of the complex, etc.

Le client peut, grâce à lui, visualiser les espaces en 3D et s’imaginer concrètement à l'intérieur de son futur appartement.
