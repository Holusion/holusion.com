Fashion Tech Festival by Showroomprivé.com and Oney

28/06/17 - 02/07/17

Oney reinventing the means of payement

The Fashion Tech Festival showcases new technologies for retailers and companies to help them in their commercial issues

During this edition of the Fashion Tech Festival, Oney used the Prism as a display solution for its new secure transaction system

The bank have developped a new way to pay, easy and efficient : A NFC tag, integrated in a smartwatch. When putting it close to a special receiver, the tag is read by the onboard computer, reconizing the user and displaying its basic information. Just a fingerprint to confirm the identity of the client, and the payement is done ! Fast, convenient, efficient, a new way to do shopping that will speed up the waiting time in lines.
