Digitize and showcase your collections

Collection digitization, conservation and mediation tools: we support museums, institutions and heritage services in showcasing architectural and cultural heritage.

Photogrammetry and laser scanning

Photogrammetry capture of collection items in inventory or directly on site

We produce and optimize faithful models of your works. Work carried out in partnership with the 3D Digitization department of the University of Lille.

{% picture grid img/photogrammetry/arc_de_triomphe_screenshot_ecorpus.webp class="img-fluid" --alt Photogrammetry of the Arc de Triomphe de l'Étoile, produced by the Centre des Monuments Nationaux and displayed on eCorpus with the participation of the Huma-Num consortium %}

Online display

Your models come to life on eCorpus, the most complete 3D viewer on the market, developed by Holusion as part of a research consortium with the universities of Lille and Liège.

{% picture grid img/services/tablette_chalons.jpg class="img-fluid" --alt A touch tablet serving as a digital label for presenting a column statue in Châlons-en-Champagne %}

On a touch screen

Present your collections on a touch screen that lets your visitors manipulate objects and discover integrated mediation elements: guided tours, annotations, enriched media...

Or augment your hologram with a remote touch screen.

Your hologram directly leverages the mediation scenarios configured on eCorpus to offer an immersive experience of great depth.

Discover the Grands Sites Archéologiques collection of the French Ministry of Culture or the collections of the National Archaeology Museum

On a touch table

Integrate eCorpus on a touch table to offer a complete and intuitive viewing experience.

A tailor-made staging

Casings, display stands and lighting are scenographic assets inseparable from a successful experience.

Our network of local manufacturers lets us design and build custom-made accessories on demand: wood, metal or plastic casings, as well as custom electronic circuits for ambient lighting, illuminated logos or visual indicators.

Because every project is unique, contact us to find the solution best suited to each of them.
